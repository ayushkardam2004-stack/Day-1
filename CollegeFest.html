About Event

College Fest 2026 is a grand celebration where students showcase their talents in cultural, technical, and fun events. It is a platform for creativity, competition, and entertainment.

Event Categories

  • Singing Competition
  • Dance Battle (Solo & Group)
  • Coding Challenge (Hackathon)
  • Debate Competition
  • Photography Contest
  • Quiz Competition

Event Schedule

  1. 09:00 AM – Registration & Welcome
  2. 10:00 AM – Inauguration Ceremony
  3. 11:00 AM – Cultural Events (Dance, Singing, Drama)
  4. 01:00 PM – Lunch Break
  5. 02:00 PM – Technical Events (Coding, Quiz, Debate)
  6. 04:00 PM – Gaming Tournament Finals
  7. 05:30 PM – Prize Distribution Ceremony
  8. 06:30 PM – Closing & DJ Night

Rules

  • All participants must carry valid college ID.
  • Respect all participants and organizers.
  • No unfair means or cheating allowed.
  • Be present at least 30 minutes before event start.
  • Decision of judges will be final.
  • Maintain discipline in the campus.
